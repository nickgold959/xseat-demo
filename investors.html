THE INVESTMENT
THESIS

Four structural conditions converge in Texas that do not replicate elsewhere at the same density or scale.

01
Existing Infrastructure

390+ cinema venues across Texas. 674,000 seats already built, wired for audio and projection, with existing operational teams. No capital deployment required for the physical network. The venue layer is already live.

02
Unabsorbed Demand

Texas has no legal sports betting. No tolerated pick-em formats. Fantasy products don't resolve live moments. Bars cannot resolve outcomes collectively. 13.5M sports fans with disposable income and no structured participation channel.

03
Sport Density

Dallas Cowboys (NFL), NASCAR (36 races), PBR (20–30 events), UFC (6–10 nights). 70–80 high-demand PRIME dates per year — more than any comparable U.S. market. Frequency drives the revenue model.

04
Structural Capture

This is not an aspirational model dependent on behaviour change. Texas fans are already organising around live sport — in bars, in living rooms, in informal watch groups. S.E.A.T formalises existing behaviour into premium attendance.
