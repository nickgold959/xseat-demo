SEAT-OSX operates outside broadcast viewing metrics, modelling and screen-based measurement frameworks. It is not a broadcast product. It does not appear in broadcast audience impressions — generating less than 0.002% of total global broadcast audience impact, contractually aligned to existing IMG commercial frameworks.
